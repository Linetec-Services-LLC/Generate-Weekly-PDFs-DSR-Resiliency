| T-04-23 | Information Disclosure | anon key in the public bundle | accept | the anon key is public by design; RLS (`current_user_role()` + role-aware policies, Phase 03) is the data guard, not key secrecy |

| T-04-25 | Spoofing | password-reset open redirect on previews | mitigate | Supabase Auth Redirect-URL allowlist (`*.vercel.app/auth/reset` + production) confirmed in the operator checkpoint (Task 3, cross-ref Plan 02) |
